film Wonder woman. According to the plot, the events of the film moved from the time of the first world war to 1984 in which Diana lives among people

PEDRO PASCAL AS MAXWELL LORD


Pedro Pascal will try on the role of one of the film's antagonists, Maxwell Lord.
Maxwell is an influential businessman who is looking for magical artifacts that will help him gain the power of God. For his quest to achieve the goal, he asks for help
from the archaeologist Barbara. However, after one of the artifacts turns her into a Cheetah, she wants to get back at him. Maxwell asks Diana for help, and in return,
he promises to resurrect Steve Trevor.
