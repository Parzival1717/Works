Wonder Woman
1984

A sequel from Warner Bros. and Director Patty Jenkins takes
you back to the ' 80s. In the center of the story is a businessman Maxwell Lord,
who is looking for artifacts to become the most powerful man on earth.
However, one of the artifacts found turns archaeologist Barbara into a Cheetah.
She wants revenge for this. However, Maxwell asks for Diana's protection, and in return,
he promises that he will return to her his beloved Steve Trevor,
who died in the First World war.
